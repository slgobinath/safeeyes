# Digital Eye Health Guide\n\n## 20-20-20 Rule\nLook at something 20 feet away every 20 minutes for 20 seconds.\n\n## Ergonomics\n- Monitor 20-30 inches away, top at eye level.\n- Chair with back support, feet flat, elbows 90°.\n- Ambient lighting softer than screen, avoid glare.\n\n## Screen Settings\n- Brightness matches room light.\n- Use warm color temperature (≈3400K) in evenings.\n- Increase font size to at least 12pt.\n- Enable blue‑light filter or night mode.\n\n## Long-Term Habits\n- Eye exams every 1-2 years.\n- Blink frequently, use lubricating drops if dry.\n- Spend 2+ hours outdoors daily.\n- Stay hydrated, eat omega‑3 rich foods.\n- Use Pomodoro technique to take regular breaks.\n\nHappy viewing!
